About

More About Me.

I am a Senior User Experience Designer with a passion for creating ways to inspire people. I am constantly seeking opportunities to solve problems, simplify situations and help people learn.

I believe as UX Designers, we have a unique opportunity to help contribute to a better designed future. I am honored to help alleviate problems, and pioneer new ways of accomplishing goals for users around the world. I have a strong passion for inspiring people, and designing ways to let them interact with information in deeper and more meaningful ways.

In addition to my design passion, I have many other interests. My second love will always be cooking. I think of the grocery store as my playground, and the kitchen as my laboratory. When I am not working on designs, you can often find me in the local Asian market, or at home creating something I have never tried to make. I also think the world of eSports is incredibly fascinating, and find myself enjoying different games when time allows.

Thanks for taking the time to get to know a bit about me. If you are curious to know more about something specific, or would just like to chat, feel free to reach out! I look forward to hearing from you.

- Theo Thompson

Services

What I Do.

As a creative, our work goes beyond pushing pixels. Designing interfaces is my main passion, but not my only one.

  • UI/UX Design

    I've had the opportunity to design for a number companies leading their respective industries. I love immersing myself in users problems, and empathizing with their painpoints and goals. Designing innovative and simplified solutions for users around the world makes me feel like I am doing my part in designing a better future. I find incredible beauty in the vast complexity that goes into creating a seemingly simple interaction.

  • Product Development

    I love designing game-changing products which delight users and simplify aspects of daily life. I've spent time designing and prototyping digital products for web, mobile, VR, and more.

  • Content Strategy

    From user research and persona/journey development, through strategy creation and designing content matrices. I enjoy taking a strategic perspective when discovering an audience. I find immersing yourself in both client and customer helps us become more effective designers.

  • Culture Building

    Working somewhere is more than just showing up everyday. Valuing and investing in culture and capabilities evolves an organization into something it could not become otherwise. I have taken initiatives to help develop culture building programs in all of the organizations I team up with.
